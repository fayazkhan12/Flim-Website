About Us

SK Enterprises, founded by Shamshad Pathan, is a leading film distribution company based in Mumbai. With over a decade of experience, we specialize in theatrical releases, syndication, and film promotion. Shamshad Pathan, a renowned film director, distributor, and syndicator, has been associated with the Bollywood film industry for a long time.

He started his career with the Hindi film 'Us Disha Mein' in 2013 and gained expertise in film marketing, distribution, and syndication. His notable work includes the film Page 16, which was widely talked about. Shamshad has successfully marketed, distributed, and syndicated numerous Bollywood films across single screens, multiplexes, national television, YouTube, DTH, OTT, and other digital platforms.

Our Services

Theatrical Distribution

We facilitate the distribution of your film to both multiplexes and independent theaters across the country, maximizing the number of screens and expanding audience access.

Syndication

We distribute the digital rights for Hindi and regional language films, web series, short films, songs, and documentaries across various platforms, including OTT, YouTube, and other digital channels. Additionally, we handle satellite rights, DTH and cable rights, as well as all forms of dubbing rights on a global scale, either individually or in bulk.

Promotion

We advocate for your film among exhibitors, sub-distributors, and syndication firms, guaranteeing optimal exposure and success.
